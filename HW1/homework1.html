I'm originally from the midwest (Ohio), spent many years in Japan and now call the Bay Area home. I live in north Oakland with my husband and work in the City. In our spare time, we enjoy going to area festivals. Some of our favorites are the San Leandro Cherry Festival, the Bay Area Book Festival (downtown Berkeley), the Northern California Cherry Blossom Festival and Parade (Japantown), Palo Alto Arts Festival, Castro Street Fair, Alameda County Fair (Pleasanton) and San Jose's Christmas in the Park.

My path to web-development has been in burst when needed at a particular moment - (compressing) and encoding videos for streaming, distributing documents, listing a directory of contacts when needed, etc. My absolute (long-time) favorite website is flightaware.com This website aggregates and flight data for almost all aircraft around the world and visualizes the information dynamically in real time on a map. To me, it is an amazingly transparent representation of how interconnected our world is - I love it!

City College offers four types of counseling services for students. Academic Counseling: this office provides guidance on degree and certificate requirements, in addition to personal, academic and career development. Career Counseling: Open to both students and alumni, this office assist constituents with career exploration and planning. Transfer Counseling: here is where constituents can learn about the requirement to transfer to a baccalaureate institution. Disabled Students Programs and Services (DSPS): this office works to allow disabled students to thrive by providing assistance and accommodation for a student's disability. Find more details, including contact information and office hours here.
